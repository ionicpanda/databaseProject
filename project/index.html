Why Study Music History?

Music history is significant; it has value. According to Anthony Storr, "music exalts life, enhances life and gives it meaning. Great music outlives the individual who created it. It is both personal and beyond the personal. For those who love it, it remains as a fixed point of reference in an unpredictable world. Music is a source of reconciliation, exhilaration, and hope which never fails."

You can never be a truly accomplished musician until you have a thorough understanding of things like music theory and music history.
